The Lotus Jacket comes in 3 beautiful different colours and is suitable for even the most windy and rainy weather.

The hood is designed for maximum comfort with soft inner lining that won’t effect your ability to hear the beautiful nature around you.

The large pockets are designed with all you adventure needs in mind, large enough to hold a map and fully waterproof.

Inspired by nature our unique waterproof fabric is both extremely water repellent and breathable. Like the lotus leaf, beads of water will simply run away from you.

This jacket is what we like to call an all round jacket. Suitable for rain, wind and breathable enough that it is even comfortable while the sun is shining.
