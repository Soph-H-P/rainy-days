Our Ethics

At RainyDays we hold the ethics of the company to the highest standards. We have partnered with charity in Botswana which supports vulnerable women and children. Our inspiring seamstresses not only receive a stable income but also extra support for the whole family. Happy workers produce happy clothing, and happy clothing leads to happy customers. So wear your gear with pride knowing you are helping improve the lives of others.

Our Technology

Rainproofing like no other. RainyDays has created it’s very own innovative fabric technology, based on that of the lotus leaf. The lotus leaf is one of natures most water resistant surfaces. This is achieved by a lecture that traps tiny amounts of air close to the surface, creating a superhydrophobic area that not only repels water but also dirt.

Comfort Zone

We don’t merely want you to be comfortable during all of life’s adventures, but we also strive to make your wallet comfortable! This is why our products come with a lifetime guarantee and free repair services. But this isn’t just cost effective it also gives the environment some comfort by reducing waste. If for some reason you are ready to change your jacket, we also offer recycling services.
